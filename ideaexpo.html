Idea Expo

Description

You and your team will have the opportunity to present innovative ideas or projects related to mechanical engineering. Display your creativity, problem-solving skills, and technical knowledge as you showcase your concept to a panel of judges


Rules

  • Teams must consist of two members for this event
  • Each team must present their ideas or project
  • Plagiarism or presenting existing ideas will lead to disqualifiacation
  • The decision of the judging panel will be final

Note: Entry fee would be Rs.100 per person
