Mech Quiz

Description

You and your team will be challenged with a series of mechanical engineering related questions. The team which answers the most number of questions would win.


Rules

  • Teams must consist of two members for this event
  • Only registered teams will be allowed to participate this event
  • Any form of cheating or misconduct will result in immediate disqualification
  • The decision of the judging panel will be final

Note: Entry fee would be Rs.100 per person
