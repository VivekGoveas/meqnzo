Paper Presentation

Description

You and your team will present a research paper on a mechanical engineering topic of your choice. Showcase your research, analytical skills, and presentation abilities as you communicate your findings to a panel of judges.


Rules

  • Teams must consist of three members for this event
  • Each presentatiion should be original and innovative
  • Time limits will be strictly followed
  • The decision of the judging panel will be final
  • Teams involving in professional misconduct will be disqualified

Note: Entry fee would be Rs.100 per person
