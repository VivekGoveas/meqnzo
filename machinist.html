Machinist

Description

You will be given a workpiece. Along with your team member you should machine the workpiece as per the given dimensions.


Rules

  • There will be a total of two rounds
  • This event has only individual participation
  • Judges result is the final
  • Final judgement will be based on the accuracy, surface finish and time consumed
  • Teams involving in professional misconduct will be disqualified

Note: Entry fee would be Rs.100 per person
